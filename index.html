Gonzalez Group

Enthusiastically Advancing AI Systems at UC Berkeley

Joseph E. Gonzalez's group conducts research in AI systems, including agentic systems, data systems, efficent training and inference, and generative model evaluation. His lab has spawned many successful projects such as Gorilla, Chatbot Arena, vLLM, MemGPT (new Letta), SGLang and more.

More information on current projects and publications can be found here. His students also have a podcast on PhD life here.
